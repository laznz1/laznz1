During O-Week we asked you what you thought of MIT wanting to Reduce/Remove the amount of Sugar available for purchase at MIT sites - A whopping 190 answered: Over all three sites, 61% agreed with MIT going sugar-free. make sure you have your say by contacting us Full results available - Here

Student Council Meeting July

Meeting Updates by Laurence Earl July 13, 2017
Hi MIT, Today we had the monthly Student Council meeting here is a quick run down on topics discussed
Sugarfree at MIT
Is a project which Diane King and her team presented to us. The initiative would remove sugared and sweetened drinks from being sold at MIT in the Vending machines and the Canteens. The Student Council want your opinion on this before we make a decision if we would like to support this.

O-Week Plans
Mel Ngata and her team have been hard at work creating an awesome and interactive stall for everyone during O-Week make sure you check it out and get your photo taken at the free photo booth!

MIT Placement Issues
The Student Council have been made aware of some select students with concerns over the MIT placements. Please contact us directly using the contact us form or make sure you fill out your digital diaries!

MIT Apparel
Jess has been working tirelessly for over a year now in order to get these completed, They are now available for purchase at Digitees (There is no mark up on these products) Well Done Jess!
