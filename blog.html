MIT Student Council

Our Goals

Ensure that students are well represented and supported across the wider Manukau Institute of Technology. We aim to support the delivery of:

  • Encourage world-class learning and teaching practices.
  • Promote outstanding student experiences and learning environments.
  • Develop adequate levels of student support.
  • Ensure there are no unfair barriers to education.
  • Promote equitable access and support for achievement.
  • Ensure students are valued members of an academic community that contributes to the creation and dissemination of knowledge.

Our Values & Priorities

Develop leaders

Foster strong relationships within student bodies (Student Council and Student Executive Voice); coaching and developing members to create influential and effective leadership amongst the student population

Deliver the message

Ensure effective representation is maintained with the highest level of professionalism and communication. Be transparent; communicate the student voice directly to the institute and back again. Turn up to all meetings and being an active participant. Developing tools to measure feedback channels to ensure needs are being met

Be the change

Valuing diversity and fostering a more positive and motivated culture within the student population through effective student engagement. Create a strong sense of identity and relatedness, where students are proud to be part of Manukau Institute of Technology

Bridge the gap

Establish effective working relationships with institute staff members; working collaboratively to help MIT achieve their strategic goals. Act in the Interest of All Manukau Institute of Technology Students, with focus on Priority groups providing students the opportunities to engage about decisions effecting them

Be Kind, Be Real, Be There

Know your students and know their struggle. Be genuine and support them through their learning experience and life in general. Offer them the right support, guide them to the right places and do everything you can to help them succeed

Own it

Take ownership of issues, follow up and deliver

Build it

Build sustainable models focusing on social, economic, environmental and personal change
