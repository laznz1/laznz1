Chairperson Blog

Ensure that students are well represented and supported across the wider Manukau Institute of Technology. We aim to support the delivery of:

  • Encourage world-class learning and teaching practices.
  • Promote outstanding student experiences and learning environments.
  • Develop adequate levels of student support.
  • Ensure there are no unfair barriers to education.
  • Promote equitable access and support for achievement.
  • Ensure students are valued members of an academic community that contributes to the creation and dissemination of knowledge.
